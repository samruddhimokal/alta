allta

Located in Dublin’s vibrant docklands, Allta offers a refined yet laid-back dining experience, celebrating Ireland’s best producers. With a warm, thoughtfully designed space, it’s perfect for a relaxed lunch or an intimate evening.

By day, the bar is a bright, airy lunch and coffee spot; by night, it transforms into a moody, intimate cocktail bar. Featuring a zero-waste philosophy, fresh oysters, and live DJs, the bar creates a vibrant night-time experience.

restaurant

Nestled in Dublin’s vibrant docklands, Allta offers a refined yet laid-back dining experience, celebrating the very best of Ireland's local producers. The warm, thoughtfully designed space exudes a welcoming energy, where every detail—from the ambient lighting to the open kitchen—invites you to unwind and indulge.

Whether you’re here for a relaxed lunch or a more intimate evening gathering, Allta’s focus on quality ingredients and carefully crafted dishes will take you on a culinary journey that blends sophistication with comfort.

bar

Bright, light, and airy lunch and coffee spot by day, dark, moody, and intimate cocktail and beer bar by night.

A cocktail journey to the islands of Ireland, focused on our zero-waste philosophy and the connection between bartender and chef. Served alongside Patrick O'Sullivan’s Cromane oysters from our oyster bar and our allta burger with hand minced 40 day aged woodtown jersey patty, no messin bakery fermented potato bun and shiitake miso butter.

Live DJs until late Thursday, Friday, and Saturday on our bespoke Toby Hatchett sound system.

Our bottleshop stocks our full wine list at retail prices, along with beers, spirits, and house-made premixed cocktails.
